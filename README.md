ValleyHike Nepal

ValleyHike Nepal is a responsive and user-friendly website designed to help hikers explore beautiful trails around the Kathmandu Valley. The website provides information about hiking routes, safety tips, scenic photos, an interactive map, and a contact form for visitors to reach out. Its goal is to make hiking easy, enjoyable, and eco-friendly while promoting local tourism.

Features

Detailed information about popular hiking trails: Shivapuri, Chisapani, Champadevi, Nagarkot, Lakuri Bhanjyang, Bishnudwar, Seto Gumba, and more.

Hiking and safety tips for beginners and experienced hikers.

Photo gallery to visualize hiking locations and scenic views.

Interactive Google Map showing all major hiking areas.

Contact form for guidance, questions, or feedback.

Fully responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Website Structure

Header & Navigation: Logo, website name, and links to all sections.

Home: Welcome message and brief introduction.

About: Purpose of the website and eco-friendly hiking message.

Trails: Highlights famous trails with images and short descriptions.

Map: Embedded Google Map with all hiking locations.

Tips: Safety and hiking guidelines for a secure adventure.

Gallery: Images of trails and scenic spots.

Blog: Detailed day hikes including difficulty, duration, season, and eco tips.

Contact: Form to send messages and inquiries.

Footer: Quick links, contact info, and website description.

Tech Stack

HTML5 – Structure

CSS3 – Styling and layout

JavaScript – Interactivity (form validation, etc.)
